# Code of Conduct

PHOTO-CAT is a scientific software project. Participation should remain professional, respectful, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected behaviour

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and constructively
- keep discussions focused on the issue, pull request, or scientific/software problem being addressed
- be clear when reporting bugs or proposing changes
- credit the work of others appropriately
- avoid harassment, insults, discriminatory language, or personal attacks

## Unacceptable behaviour

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, intimidation, personal attacks, discriminatory comments, deliberate disruption of discussions, or sharing private information without permission.

## Reporting

If a behaviour issue occurs, contact the project maintainers privately through the repository owner or the contact channel listed by the project.

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, block users, or take other reasonable actions to keep the project usable and professional.
